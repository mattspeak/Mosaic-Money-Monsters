- Accessibility: MoneyTime can be accessed from any computer or tablet with internet connectivity, making it suitable for various learning environments, including schools, homes, and distance learning.

- Practical Learning: The curriculum emphasizes real-world applications, encouraging students to learn by doing, which enhances retention and understanding of financial principles.

MoneyTime recognizes that young people benefit from engaging and practical learning experiences. By providing a self-paced and interactive platform, the program aligns with the preferences of today's youth, who often favor technology-driven solutions for education.
